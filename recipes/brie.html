Baked Brie

This Brie cheese recipe wraps a whole wheel of Brie cheese filled with preserves in puff pastry. It's baked until the pastry is golden and crisp and Brie is warm and gooey inside for a cheesy treat.

How to Bake Brie

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Baked Brie Ingredients

These are the four ingredients you'll need to make this easy baked brie recipe:

  • Brie: Start with an 8-ounce wheel of Brie cheese.
  • Preserves: Use your favorite type of preserves â€” we recommend peach or apricot. You can also use your favorite jam.
  • Puff pastry: You'll need one sheet of puff pastry, thawed.
  • Egg white: Brush the puff pastry with an egg white before baking to give it a shiny finish.

How to Make Baked Brie

Here's a brief overview of what you can expect when you bake brie at home:

  1. Slice Brie cheese wheel in half so that you have two circles of cheese.
  2. Sandwich the preserves between the cheese halves, then reassemble.
  3. Wrap the entire wheel of Brie with the puff pastry.
  4. Transfer onto the prepared baking sheet seam-side down.
  5. Brush puff pastry with egg white.
